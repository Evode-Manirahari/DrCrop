How the audit works

Three steps. Two flights. One signed prescription.

  1. 1

    We fly one block

    You pick a block — usually 5–40 acres of under-vine weed pressure. A Part 107 pilot flies an RGB drone in a 2–4 hour window. No equipment, no software for you to install.

  2. 2

    AI segments. A PCA signs.

    DrCrop processes the orthomosaic into a red/yellow/green map: spray, scout, skip. A California-licensed PCA reviews and signs the prescription before it lands in your hands.

  3. 3

    We prove the savings

    After your spray pass, we re-fly. You get a verification report: acres skipped, chemical saved, and confirmation the unsprayed acres stayed healthy. If we can't show savings, you don't pay.
