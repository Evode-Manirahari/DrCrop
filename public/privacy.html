Privacy

What we collect and how we use it.

Effective 2026-05-22.

The short version

  • We collect what you give us through the audit signup form: name, vineyard, email, phone, county, block acres, current herbicide program, fly window, notes.
  • We use it to contact you about the audit you requested. That's it.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your contact info with anyone.
  • You own the imagery and prescription maps we produce from your block. We keep a copy so we can re-fly and verify savings.
  • You can ask us to delete everything we have on you. Email privacy@drcrop.example and we'll do it within 14 days.

What we collect

From the audit form: name, role, vineyard name, county, block acres, current under-vine herbicide program, email, phone, preferred fly window, and any notes you add. We also capture the user-agent string your browser sends, which is used only for debugging and abuse prevention.

From the flight itself: orthomosaic imagery of the block you authorize us to fly, the field boundary you provide, and the prescription map we produce. We store these so we can re-fly the same block for verification.

Not collected: we do not run analytics, set advertising cookies, or fingerprint your device. There is no third-party tracking script on this site.

How we use it

  • To contact you about the audit you requested.
  • To deliver the prescription map and verification report.
  • To improve the underlying segmentation model — only your imagery, never your contact info, and only when we have a written data-use agreement with you for the season.

Who we share it with

We share your imagery and prescription map with one party: the California-licensed PCA who reviews and signs your prescription. The PCA is bound by their own professional confidentiality obligations.

We share your contact info with no one. No vendors, no marketing partners, no investors. Internal use only.

Two narrow exceptions we hope never apply: (1) if we're legally compelled by a subpoena or court order, and (2) if you explicitly ask us to (for example, "send my prescription map to my applicator").

How we store it

Audit signups are stored in a single append-only file on our server (`data/audit-signups.jsonl`). Imagery is stored in object storage with access limited to DrCrop staff and the assigned PCA. We don't encrypt-at-rest yet because we're a one-person shop — that changes when we scale past 10 paying customers.

How long we keep it

Signup info: indefinitely, unless you ask us to delete it. Imagery: for the duration of our engagement plus 24 months, so we can compare against future flights. We delete imagery sooner on request.

Your rights

  • Access: ask what we have on you, we'll send the full record within 14 days.
  • Correction: tell us what's wrong, we fix it.
  • Deletion: ask us to delete everything, we do it within 14 days. (Some accounting records may survive if we've sent you an invoice — California tax requirements.)
  • Portability: we'll export your data as JSON if you ask.

Email privacy@drcrop.example for any of these. California residents have additional CCPA rights — same email, same response window.

Changes

If we change this notice in a way that affects how we use your data, we'll email you before the change takes effect. Cosmetic changes don't trigger an email.

Contact

Questions: privacy@drcrop.example. Real person, single inbox, replies within 2 business days.
